# Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment (FRIA)
**Article 27 Compliance**

## 1. Intended Purpose
iLuminara is a High-Risk AI System (Class A) used for critical health logistics.

## 2. Groups at Risk
* Refugees (Vulnerable Population)
* Local Health Workers (Data Subjects)

## 3. Mitigation Measures
* **Human-in-the-Loop:** All kinetic actions require CHW confirmation.
* **Explainability:** CoT outputs exposed for every decision.
* **Right to Redress:** Transparency Portal allows contesting automated decisions.
