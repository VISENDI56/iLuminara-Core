Field Observation Log: Dadaab & Kalobeyei
Date: Oct 15-20, 2025
Observer: Lead Architect
Subject: CHW Anxiety & Workflow

## Key Findings
* Cognitive Overload: CHWs ignore apps that require >4 clicks.
* Connectivity Anxiety: Workers fear losing data when the "spinning wheel" appears.
  * Solution: "Optimistic UI" - show "Saved" immediately, sync later.
* Language Switching: Field workers switch between Swahili and Somali mid-sentence.
  * Solution: Toggle button always visible on top-right.

## Journey Map: "The Fever Case"
* Trigger: Child presents with high fever.
* Pain Point: Connectivity drops. CHW panics.
* iLuminara Intervention: App works offline. RetinaInput reads handwritten card.
* Result: Anxiety reduced. Diagnosis logged.
