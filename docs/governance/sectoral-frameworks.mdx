---
title: 'Sectoral Frameworks'
description: 'The 29 Context-Specific Compliance Extensions.'
---

The Sectoral 29 are specialized frameworks enforced only when specific triggers are met. These are managed by the `sectoral_laws.json` and the Governance Kernel.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Supply Chain (UFLPA, CSDDD, LkSG)">
    - UFLPA: U.S. Uyghur Forced Labor Prevention Act
    - CSDDD: EU Corporate Sustainability Due Diligence Directive
    - LkSG: German Supply Chain Act
    - **Trigger:** `if origin == "XUAR": BLOCK`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Finance (OFAC, FATF, AML)">
    - OFAC: U.S. Office of Foreign Assets Control
    - FATF: Financial Action Task Force
    - AML: Anti-Money Laundering
    - **Trigger:** `if payee in SANCTIONS_DB: FREEZE`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="ESG (CBAM, Paris 6.2)">
    - CBAM: EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism
    - Paris 6.2: Paris Agreement Article 6.2
    - **Trigger:** `calculate_carbon_tax(logistics)`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Pharma (EU MDR, FDA Part 11)">
    - EU MDR: EU Medical Device Regulation
    - FDA Part 11: U.S. FDA Electronic Records
    - **Trigger:** `log_post_market_surveillance()`
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
