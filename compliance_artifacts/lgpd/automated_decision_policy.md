# Política de Revisão de Decisões Automatizadas (LGPD Art. 20)

**Data Controller:** iLuminara Sovereign Health
**Jurisdiction:** Brazil (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados)

## 1. Right to Review (Direito à Revisão)
In compliance with Article 20 of the LGPD, any user subject to an automated health risk assessment has the right to request a review.

## 2. Explanation Mechanism (CoT)
iLuminara utilizes a "Chain-of-Thought" (CoT) engine to provide transparency.
- **Criteria Used:** Symptom data, location risk, and vital signs.
- **Procedures:** The AI logic trace is available in the user dashboard under "Diagnostic Reasoning."

## 3. Human Intervention
A human health officer (The Soldier) reviews all "High-Risk" classifications before a lockdown order is enforced.
